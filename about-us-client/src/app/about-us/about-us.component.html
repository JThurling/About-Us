Our Story


Car Assistance Network (CAN) (Pty) Ltd was established in 2003 with the vision of helping people during roadside emergencies: providing assistance through traumatising situations in the most cost-efficient way, whilst upholding the highest level of quality service.

With over 15 years of experience in the emergency services industry, we have done business with corporate companies, brokers, insurance companies, underwriting managers and currently, work with over 2000 brokers nationwide.
